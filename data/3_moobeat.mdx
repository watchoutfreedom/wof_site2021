<h4>Moobeat is an open software developed with Unity and PureData which purpose is to play music and make art.</h4>

<h4><a href="http://www.moobeat.com">Moobeat</a></h4> is an instru-game. A new app created together with <a href="http://www.noon-interactive.com">Noon</a> to make art through body movements. Music, paintings and sculpture controlled using any motion capture camera.
<div> <iframe width="70%" height="450" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/rs4w7ZXfc0s" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe></div>

<a href="http://www.moobeat.com">Moobeat: Instrugame</a> and <a href="http://moobeat.com/snstsia/">Moobeat: SNSTsia</a> are the two main variations of the app. Both entertain and help individuals improve their mental, physical, and sensory condition.

<img class="" src="http://localhost:8888/wp/wp-content/uploads/2016/10/Moobeat-casa-1-1024x683.jpg" alt="moobeat-casa" width="1024" height="683" />

It was born in 2012 and has since then been selected by <a href="https://sonar.es/">Sonar '14</a>, <a href="http://www.zincshower.com/">Zinc Shower '14</a>, <a href="http://www.blogyuzz.org/2015/02/moobeat-mejorar-a-base-de-musica-conoce-al-iii-ganador-yuzz-2014/">Yuzz</a><a href="http://www.blogyuzz.org/2015/02/moobeat-mejorar-a-base-de-musica-conoce-al-iii-ganador-yuzz-2014/"> '14</a> and other events as an innovative music creation tool. The software is now available <a href="http://www.moobeat.com">online</a> so anyone can download and try it at home (you need a Kinect 1 camera). Next step? Maybe Xbox ;-)
